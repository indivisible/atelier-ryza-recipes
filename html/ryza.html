Help

Atelier Ryza 1/2 item database

Start typing item or category name in the 'Name' field, to search for. You can also search for items by effects. For example to search for gases that can have effecct spread enter "gas" in the Name field, and "spread" in the Effect field.

You can also filter the results by checking one or more elements you want the item to (possibly) have.

Bugs / missing features

  • DLC items are not available at the moment
  • Chain finding is missing
  • Full recipe display is missing
